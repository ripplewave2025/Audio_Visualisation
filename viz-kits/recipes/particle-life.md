## Idea
N particle types; attraction matrix `M[i][j]` drives force. Emergent “cells”, worms, DNA, etc.

## Automate
1. Open presets in `viz-kits/data/particle-life-models/` for inspiration (dna, worm, flower_cell…).
2. Map matrix gains → `lifeForce`, species count → `lifeSpecies`.
3. Audio: 808 → force scale, hats → chaos, BPM → integration phase.
