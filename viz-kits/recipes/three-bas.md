# Kit: three-bas

**Upstream:** `vendor/three-bas` (MIT)  
**Ready:** `viz-kits/ready/lib/bas.module.js`, `viz-kits/ready/shaders/bas-glsl/` (36 GLSL eases/splines)

## Idea
Buffer Animation System — animate thousands of prefab meshes/points with GLSL timelines. Great for crystal shards, morphs, smear.

## Best examples
- `vendor/three-bas/examples/audio_visual/`

- `vendor/three-bas/examples/gpgpu/`
